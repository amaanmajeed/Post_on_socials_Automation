Hacktoberfest is a global event with the main goal to raise awareness about Open Source and encourage as many developers as possible to participate regularly, not just in October. Hacktoberfest also comes with a challenge and invites participants to complete it. Participate in HacktoberFest and get a chance to redeem an official HacktoberFest 2022 T-shirt or get a tree planted in your name.
